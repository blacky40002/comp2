"Well," said Thorndyke, "as I have finished with the case, and am handing it over to you, I will put the evidence before you _seriatim_. Now, I think we are agreed that, at the moment when the blow was struck, the deceased was standing before the fireplace, winding the clock. The dagger entered obliquely from the left, and, if you recall its position, you will remember that its hilt pointed directly towards an open window."
