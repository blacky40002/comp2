I reflected for a few moments. “Let me see,” said I. “It was a good while ago, and I remember that it was a Saturday, because the men who were filling the holes in the city wall had knocked off at noon for a week-end. Now when was it? I went to the wine merchant’s that day, and--” I paused with a sudden shock of recollection. “Why!” I exclaimed. “It was _the_ Saturday; the day Mrs. Frood disappeared!”
