This was dated three months before. Wilson put the few remaining bits of this letter in his pocket. Was it possible that this grinning idol which already had played so important a part in his own life was the one mentioned here? And the priest of whom Sorez spoke--could it be he who ruled these tribes in the Andes? It was possible--Lord, yes, _anything_ was possible. But none of these things hinted as to where the girl now was.
