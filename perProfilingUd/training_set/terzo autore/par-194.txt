Kingswell felt the deck jump under his feet. His pulses leaped with the good planks. "Hit!" he cried--and sure enough, one of the enemy's upper spars, with its burden of flapping canvas, tottered desperately, and then swooped down on the clustered buccaneers beneath. Half an hour later the _Heart of the West_ was spinning along on her old course, and far astern the stranger lay to and nursed her wound.
