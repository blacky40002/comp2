D'Antons was cordially welcomed aboard the _Cristobal_. Miwandi was received without question. The acting commander of the ship was a grizzled Spanish mariner by the name of Silva,--a fellow steeped in crime and uncertain of temper, yet possessed of a marvellous devotion for D'Antons, which was due to an act of kindness performed by the Frenchman years before, in the town of Panama.
