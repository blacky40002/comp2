So the chase was carried on, as has been described in a previous chapter, and went merrily enough for the _Cristobal_ until the unexpected shot from the stern of the quarry brought down her foretopmast and its weight of sail. But before that had happened, D'Antons, unrecognizable himself in new clothes and a great hat, marked Bernard Kingswell on the poop of the _Heart of the West_. He cursed like a madman, or a true-bred pirate, when his ship was crippled.
