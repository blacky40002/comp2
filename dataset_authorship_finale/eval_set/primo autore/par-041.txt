"Not a great deal," replied Thorndyke. "The character is the Moabite or Phoenician--primitive Semitic, in fact--and reads from right to left. The language I take to be Hebrew. At any rate, I can find no Greek words, and I see here a group of letters which _may_ form one of the few Hebrew words that I know--the word _badim_, 'lies.' But you had better get it deciphered by an expert."
