"You fool!" he cried, glaring. "What d'you mean by it? So you are on Wigmore's side, are you?--on the side of the man who tried to murder his servant and me--yes, and who marked and dealt those cursed cards! You'd sit on _my_ back, would you? For two pins I'd pick you up and heave you against the wall. Tell me--were you in league with this old devil? Tell me quick--or I'll finish you! Did you know Wigmore was marking those cards?"
